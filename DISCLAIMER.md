## Requirements

Full functionality requires:

1. A Mac with **Apple Silicon** (M1/M2/M3/M4 or later)
2. **macOS 13** or newer (recommended)
3. An active **SuperGrok** or **X Premium Plus** subscription
4. The **Grok Build CLI** installed at `~/.grok/bin/grok`

Without the Grok Build CLI and an active subscription, the app will launch but **cannot run agent tasks**.
